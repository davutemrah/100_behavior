Chapter 2 Interview Questions

2.1 Tell me about yourself

2.1.1 Version 1

Thanks for having me this opportunity, I really appreciate your time.

I am an accomplished professional with over 5 years of experience in the field of data science and economic research. My educational background includes a Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Kansas.

My expertise lies in statistics, econometrics, and hands-on experience with machine learning and causal models with a strong foundation in economics and business intelligence.

In terms of technical skills, I am proficient in a variety of programming languages such as R, Python, and SQL, as well as big data tools like Databricks and AWS. I have advanced knowledge in causal inference methods, including but not limited to Difference-in-Differences, Instrumental Variables, Synthetic Control, Regression Discontinuity, Propensity Score Matching, and Inverse Probability Weighting (IPW). My experience extends to agile software development methodologies, with proficiency in Jira.

In my most recent role as Associate Director of Marketing Sciences at Horizon Media, - conducting descriptive and exploratory statistics to understand the audience and target consumers. - Utilizing supervised and unsupervised machine learning methods to predict business outcomes - Performing post-campaign performance analysis on consumer behaviors using causal inference models. - Developing and maintaining automated scripts shared by the team and the company on sample balancing, weighting, and weighted random sampling based on selected features to enhance insights, demographic profile reports.

Overall, my combination of academic achievements, technical skills, and practical experience uniquely position me to bring valuable insights and contribute effectively to data-driven decision-making processes.

2.1.2 Version 2

Thanks for giving me this opportunity, I am excited and appreciate your time.

I am an accomplished professional with over 5 years of experience in the field of data science and economic research. My educational background includes a Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Kansas.

My expertise lies in statistics, econometrics, and hands-on experience with machine learning and causal models.

• In terms of technical skills, I am proficient in a variety of programming languages such as R, Python, Spark, SQL, and STATA as well as big data tools like Databricks and AWS. • I have hands-on experience in causal inference methods, including but not limited to Difference-in-Differences, Instrumental Variables, Synthetic Control, Regression Discontinuity, Propensity Score Matching, and Inverse Probability Weighting (IPW).

In my current role as Associate Director of Marketing Sciences at Horizon Media, - conducting descriptive and exploratory analysis to understand the audience and target consumers. - Utilizing supervised and unsupervised machine learning methods to predict business outcomes, customer segmentation, create modeled audiences for scaling purposes - Performing post-campaign performance analysis on consumer behaviors using causal inference models. - Developed and maintained automated scripts shared by the team and the company on sample balancing, weighting, and weighted random sampling based on selected features to enhance insights and demographic profile reports.

Overall, my combination of academic achievements, technical skills, and practical experience positions me to bring valuable insights and contribute effectively to data-driven decision-making processes.

2.1.3 Relocation:

I am open to relocating, but I would need to consider my family’s needs in the process. I would appreciate more information on the support provided for families during the relocation.

2.1.4 Timeline to start:

I am very interested in this role and eager to join your team. I am currently employed and would need to give my employer two weeks’ notice. Is this timeline acceptable to you?

2.1.5 Salary:

  1. Based on my research and considering my experience and skills, I’m looking for a salary in the range of $160K to $200K that can be tailored as I learn more about the position and responsibilities.

  2. I have done some research on industry standards for this role and given my background and experience, I am looking for a salary in the range of $160K to $200K. However, I am open to discussing this further based on the overall compensation package and growth opportunities at XXXX.

  3. At this stage, I am more focused on understanding the role and how I can contribute to XXXX. I’d prefer to discuss salary once we have both determined that I am a good fit for the position and the company. I am confident that we can come to a mutually beneficial agreement when the time comes.

2.1.6 Why XXXX

• I am excited about the economist position at Amazon because of the company’s commitment to innovation and data-driven decision-making. Amazon’s scale and impact provide a unique opportunity to work on challenging problems that can significantly influence market trends and consumer behavior.

• This role aligns perfectly with my career aspirations of applying economic theories and quantitative methods to real-world business problems. At Amazon, I see the potential to contribute to strategic initiatives that enhance customer experience, leveraging data to drive impactful decisions. My background in econometric modeling and statistical analysis has prepared me well to succeed in this role and add value to Amazon’s objectives.
