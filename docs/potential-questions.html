Behavioral Interview Notes

Chapter 6 Potential Questions

About the Role

  1. Can you describe the day-to-day responsibilities of this role?

  2. What does success look like in this position, and how do you measure it?

About the Team

  1. Can you tell me more about the team I would be working with?

  2. How does this team fit into the broader organization?

About Career Development

  1. What opportunities for professional development does Amazon provide?

  2. How does Amazon support career growth and progression?

About Company Culture

  1. How would you describe the company culture at Amazon?

  2. What do you enjoy most about working at Amazon?

  3. How does Amazon promote a work-life balance?

About the Next Steps

  1. What are the next steps in the interview process?

  2. When can I expect to hear back from you?

About Challenges and Expectations

  1. What are the common challenges new hires face in this position?

  2. Are there any skills or experiences you think I should focus on improving before potentially starting this role?

About the Company’s Future

  1. What are some of the company’s goals for the next few years?

  2. How does this role contribute to Amazon’s overall objectives?

Other Practical Matters

  1. What are the typical working hours for this position?

  2. Is there any travel required for this role?
